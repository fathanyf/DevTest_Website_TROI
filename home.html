About Us

Background

As an archipelagic nation, Indonesia has many beautiful natural scenery tourism sites that can be enjoyed not only from land but also from sea and air. With this potential, it is very possible to develop extreme adventure sports activities as the main industry in the region.

Adventure-extreme sport activities such as Paragliding, Paramotor, Rafting, Hiking, Diving, Mountaint Bike, Clift climbing, and caving are considered as a good activities for this kind of tourism.

Realizing these potential, TROI was established and active since 2013 for helping developing adventure sport tourism in Indonesia.

History
It all started with an idea to promote Indonesia's natural scenic beauty through paragliding. A group of paraglider pilots came together to establish a paragliding activity series known as the Trip of Indonesia (TROI).

This activity is a paragliding competition that has been held in two cities, Manado (North Sulawesi) and Painan (West Sumatra), every year since 2014. Later, it grew to three and then five cities.

Today, TROI activities are not only held in five cities but also in certain cities and regencies such as Batang, Modangan, Trenggalek, Mandalika, Palu, Aceh, and more. Many regions in Indonesia want to host TROI activities. As a result, TROI has become a local brand that can be used by regions to attract domestic and international tourists.

Our Positioning

Purpose
  • Coordinating adventure sports activities in Indonesia.
  • Developing new destinations for adventure-extreme sport tourism in all Indonesia.
Positioning
  • Helping to develop tourism activities in the regions of Indonesia through adventure sports tourism.
  • TROI activities have been consistently active since 2013 until today (as 2023).
